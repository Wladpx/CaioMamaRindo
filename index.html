Welcome to Cat's Oven!

At Cat's Oven, we believe that the secret to a purrfect pizza lies in the passion and creativity we bring to the table—just like a cat's curiosity! Our cozy little corner of culinary heaven is more than just a restaurant; it's a place where feline finesse meets the art of pizza-making.

Our head chef, Caio Augusto, or as we fondly call him, "The Cat Whisperer of the Kitchen," has spent years perfecting his craft. With a whisker of experience and a paw-sitive attitude, Chef Caio blends traditional techniques with a playful twist. Whether it's a classic Margherita or a daring "Kitty's Delight" topped with all your favorite fixings, every slice is a masterpiece.

But what truly sets Cat's Oven apart is our dedication to quality and our love for all things cats! From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and a menu full of purr-sonality. So come in, relax, and let us serve you a slice of happiness—one that's sure to have you feline fine in no time!

About Us

Welcome to Cat’s Oven, where the love for pizza and cats comes together in purrfection! Founded in the heart of Maceió, Alagoas, our restaurant brings you a unique dining experience inspired by our favorite feline companions.

Our head chef, Caio Augusto, born and raised in Maceió, brings his passion for pizza and creative flair to every dish. With years of culinary expertise and a deep connection to his Brazilian roots, Caio infuses every slice with love and flavor.
